About Me
In a few months, I will be graduating from CCVI and starting my next life adventure, Computer Engineering. Computer Engineering combines my passion for problem-solving using programming with hands-on designing and building my ideas. As side projects, I enjoy programming with Java and have mastered Python to win the Calysto Hackathon. Inspired by the COVID vaccine storage concerns, my recent engineering prototype is a desktop fridge made from recycled materials that an Arduino regulates. Ultimately, my goal is to combine my passions and education to become a founding member of a start-up creating technology that can improve society.



Work
